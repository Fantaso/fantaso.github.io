WeFarm

Intro

WeFarm is committed to integrate renewable energy farms into the streets of the cities to produce zero mile, organic, fresh & tasty food just around the corner.

Product

Yes, you can also have ONE WeHarvester for yourself, at your house or work & have fun growing your own food & experimenting growing that thing you ate & loved when you traveled to that place.

Service

WeFarm offers a service where you can grow your own food just around the corner of your neighborhood, while WeFarm does all the dirty work, you get to have all the fun.

FOOD YEAR ROUND

Regardless of seasons, the WeHarvester is able to grow food every day of the year.

PRODUCES ITS OWN ENERGY

WeHarvester works as a semi-autonomous system, providing itself with energy & water it requires to get the job done.

HEAL THE SORROUNDINGS

All waste from the WeHarvesters are transformed into beneficial waste that goes back into its local environment to keep healthy neighborhoods.
