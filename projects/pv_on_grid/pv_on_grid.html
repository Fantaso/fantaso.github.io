Photovoltaic On-Grid Project

1.27 MW PHOTOVOLTAIC ON-GRID GROUND-MOUNTED POWER PLANT

JULY 2012

The academic project aimed to design a 1,37 MWp ground-mounted photovoltaic power plant located in Heng Chun, Taiwan.

Project requirements were to consider technical aspects such as: shading analysis and panel angle inclination optimization, system efficiencies, local grid-connection and tariff, energy yield potential, among others.
