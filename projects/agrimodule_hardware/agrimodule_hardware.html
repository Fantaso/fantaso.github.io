Agrimodule Casing Project

DESIGN, MODEL AND BUILD AGRIMODULE SENSOR CASING

AUGUST 2017

Project aimed to design, digitalize and build the first casing for the agrimodule sensor. The agrimodule's case should be able to host a solar panel on top. The solar panel should have an standard optimum angle inclination to harvest energy efficiency. The case should have enough space to place the electronic components and battery. The case should have access to place LED indicators and a power button.

tools: Fusion 360 Craftware Illustrator
