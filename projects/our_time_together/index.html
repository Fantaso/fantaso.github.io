Our Time Together

December 2019

Project aimed to build an web application that would allow couples living together to organized their belongings, responsabilities or desires that the couple shares and cares the most.

This webapp was an idea of my life-partner and I, in order to keep things we share together in one place, safe private and secure in a mini-server to be running at our house.

In this Webapp our idea to have things like movies, restaurants or places we went or would love to go or even to create a organized pictures and memories we enjoy together

For now, there are 2 applications developed: 1. A Tasks/Todo application which serves to organized the responsabilities we share and 2. a Library/BookShelf application since we have a lots of books in the house and often we seen not to find because we lend it to somebody and we do not remembered and so on..

Cool Features:

  • Bookshelf app: when adding a new book to your bookshelf app you can just introduce the ISBN-10 number from the book to retrieve the book's data automatically.
